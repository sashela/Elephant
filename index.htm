"Raj: Symbol of Triumph"

Meet Raj, the resilient elephant who defied all odds after surviving a poacher attack that left him badly wounded. Thanks to the dedicated care at our facility, Raj made a remarkable recovery, embodying the strength and spirit of our conservation efforts. Today, he thrives in a safe environment, enjoying a life filled with joy and curiosity. One of his endearing quirks is his fondness for adorning his head with leaves, a playful reminder of his indomitable nature and the hope he represents for all elephants under our care. Raj's journey is a testament to the impact of our work and the resilience of these magnificent creatures.
